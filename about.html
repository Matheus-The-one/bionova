About BioNova

At BioNova, we are committed to turning waste into a valuable resource. Our innovative technology allows farmers to transform food waste and animal waste into biogas, a clean and renewable source of energy.

How It Works

Our machines are designed to convert organic waste into biogas through a process called anaerobic digestion. By breaking down the waste in the absence of oxygen, we produce methane-rich biogas that can be used for cooking, heating, and generating electricity. This process not only provides a sustainable energy source but also helps reduce landfill waste and greenhouse gas emissions.
